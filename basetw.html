How to Play

  • Memorize the sequence of attacks shown by the monster
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons or pressing 1-4 keys
  • Correct sequences damage the monster, mistakes damage you
  • Defeat monsters to advance to higher levels
  • Healer attacks restore your health but don't damage monsters

Monster difficulty increases with each level - defeat the Dragon to win the game!
